1. DATASET COMPOSITION:
- Total Classes: 43
- Image Format: .ppm (converted to .png for pipeline)
- Mean Image Size: 32x32 pixels

2. CLASS DISTRIBUTION ANALYSIS:
- Highest Frequency: Class 1 (Speed limit 30km/h) - 2250 images
- Lowest Frequency: Class 0 (Speed limit 20km/h) - 210 images
- Action Taken: Synthetic minority oversampling implemented in training script.

- Lighting: 15% of images are "Low Light" (under 50 mean pixel intensity).

- Mitigation: Used Gaussian Blur and Brightness Augmentation in Ablation Study #1.

4. RL STATE MAPPING:
- Identified 5 critical sign groups (Prohibitory, Mandatory, Danger, Speed, Other) to simplify the RL Agent's state-space.
